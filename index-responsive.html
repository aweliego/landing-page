Concept

Want to organise an event, but not all your guests have Facebook or Whatsapp? You've come to the right place. This platform doesn't require any of your guests to be on social media or to share their numbers with you. All they need is an email address. Let you and your guests be walked through the steps of creating or joining an event, and smile at how painless it is to get it done in just a few clicks, without sacrificing the layout.

Easy to use

Simply follow our interactive guidance and be done in no time.

Our approach aims at making the whole process of creating an event and inviting guests as smooth and uncomplicated as possible. In a hurry? Then Eventful is the perfect tool for you!

Customizable

Fast doesn't mean you cannot be creative. Add your personal touch to your event and give your guests a teaser of what to expect!

We believe a customisable event is a successful event! If you wish, pick one of our themes for inspiration or create your own to give your event page the desired look to best target each type of audience.

Share in a unique way

Once created, just use the link of your event to have people check it out!

We want to give the opportunity to everyone to share their event in the way that suits them the most. No social media account? No worries, we thought of that too!
